    • üß© CHURCH AND STRUCTURE

    Our Church is multi-congregational. This means that even though we are one Church with one government, the Church is divided into local assemblies (branches).

    Each local assembly is sub-divided into home cell churches called House Fellowships.

    ‚ù§Ô∏è House Fellowships

    These are the heart and strength of our church life, where a small group of members meet every week in different homes for mutual support, Bible study and prayer. Due to their small sizes, cell Churches allow members to become better acquainted with one another. They provide an opportunity for personal care and attention not afforded by larger church meetings.

    • EVANGELISTIC/FOLLOW UP & CHURCH GROWTH MINISTRY

    The supreme task of Glorious Salvation Bible Church Inc. is Soul Winning. The Ministry of Evangelism involves every member of the Church. We have a group of dedicated believers who work with the Ministry to plan and sponsor Evangelistic outreaches.

    HEALING AND FAITH CLINIC

    This Ministry serves as a channel to bring healing and deliverance to those in need of it. We conduct healing services where we administer the teaching of faith and divine Health from God.

    HOSPITAL MINISTRY

    This Ministry serves as a channel to bring healing and deliverance to those who are sick. We also conduct healing services, and in that service, we administer the teaching of faith and divine Health from God. Sometimes, we can send a team of men and women from the Church to go to hospitals, Clinics, Maternity homes to reach out to people who are sick, so we can pray for them, and anoint them with Holy Oil in the Name of our Lord Jesus.

    PRISON MINISTRY

    This Ministry aims at reaching out to every prison in this country and abroad to assist prisoners by ministering the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ and also presenting them with clothes, food and other items.

    VISITATION MINISTRY

    This is a group of dedicated members who accomplish the work of Evangelism through visitation of our members. First-timers in our Church receive the warm hand of fellowship through their visitation. They are a friendly group of people who administer counselling, healing and restoration through their visitation.

    MISSION FIELD

    Here at Glorious Salvation Bible Church Inc., dedicated and ordained ministers are sent on mission fields to equip them for their ministerial duties or assignment after ordination.

    PRAYER/INTERCESSORY MINISTRY

    The intercessory Ministry is involved in several areas. It is the power line of all the ministries under the Glorious Salvation Bible Church Inc. The Ministry recognizes that a prayerless Christian is a powerless Christian.

    PRAISE AND WORSHIP MINISTRY

    We believe that music is a very meaningful part of our services, in that it helps us to create an atmosphere for praise and worship.
    We endeavour not to perform but to minister to those who attend or visit our congregation. This ministry will produce praise and worship songs for edification and comfort among the brethren.

    MEDIA/COMMUNICATION MINISTRY

    The Communication Ministry has its Evangelical strategies to reach the unreached through Newsletters, tracts, distribution of Bibles, tapes and other Christian literature and the medium of radio, internet and television, for blessing humanity.

    CHILDREN'S CHURCH

    A well organized, energetic programme is provided here for the children of our congregation. The children are taught the principle of God's word, worship, and praise, all at a level they understand.

    WOMEN'S MINISTRY (DEBORAH WOMEN FOR CHRIST INTL)

    There are many avenues of services tied to this group. We have a home and foreign missions projects and have retreats, and programmes where we meet with other Christian women to share fellowship as the Lord permits. Through the ministry, we encourage women to rise to the demands of their generation by stirring their callings into office in the ministry.

    YOUTH MINISTRY (DYNAMIC YOUTHS MINISTRY)

    The Youth department is a very integral part of the church. The primary purpose of this group is to prepare the youths as Gods arrow and battle axe for kingdom exploit. This is achieved by organizing youth retreats, fellowship, concerts and conferences to empower them to take their world by storm and become high flyers in life.
    The Dynamic Youths are also referred to as the Elisha Generation as the mantle or baton of leadership will be transferred to their generation as the leaders of tomorrow.

    MEN'S MINISTRY (DAVID MEN'S MINISTRY)

    Here at Glorious Salvation Bible Church Inc., men's Ministry forms an integral part of the work and as such share the objectives of the Church with its entire department. The primary reason we exist is that we might share our Christian faith in the following ways.
    FELLOWSHIP: This provides us with the opportunity in an informal way to get to know one another.
    INTRODUCTION:We value the privilege of meeting and sharing in the study of Gods word so that we might better prepare ourselves as Christian laymen. This is done through study groups, seminars, retreats and conferences.
    SERVICE: The primary purpose of our mens ministry is to harness the manpower of our Church for Christian Service through involvement. This is achieved through personal witness to our Neighbors, friends and business associates and by sharing and caring for the less privileged.
