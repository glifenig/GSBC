We are Glorious Salvation Bible Church

Glorious Salvation Bible Church Inc. started in 1990 with a mandate to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ and to establish ministries and mission work that would be patterned after the New Testament model.

The Ministries aim at providing Pastoral care, Christian fellowship and discipleship for Christians who desire to be part of the church while the Mission work is aimed at winning the world for Christ through evangelism, crusades and other outreaches.

Our Purpose.

The goal of the Church is to present the presence and person of Jesus Christ to the world to:
* Change lives in our generation
* Raise men and women who are accountable to God
* Empower those are available and accessible for Him to use them practically in their various callings.

Our Mission.

We are a church with the mandate to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ and win the world for Christ.
